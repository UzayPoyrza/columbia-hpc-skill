# Ginsburg - New Scratch Space

Source: https://columbiauniversity.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/rcs/pages/1404600359/Ginsburg+-+New+Scratch+Space

---

To ensure the long-term stability and performance of the new **Ginsburg** storage infrastructure, we have completed the initial provisioning of the new scratch space.

### Purpose of Early Provisioning

We are intentionally provisioning this space now to **stress test the new storage system**. This phase is critical for validating system performance under load. This is especially important following Phase 1, where allocations were based on specific requested requirements rather than a flat percentage of current usage.

- **Phase 1 Retirement Groups:** Your storage has been provisioned according to the **specific requirements** requested during discovery.
- **Phase 2 & 3 Retirement Groups:** You have been provisioned with **50% of your current** `/burg-archive` **quota** on the new mount.
