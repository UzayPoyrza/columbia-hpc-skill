This page will list citations for research that was aided by the Insomnia HPC cluster.

*Question:*

*How do I acknowledge the resources I have used on Insomnia, Ginsburg, and Manitou in publications?*

*Answer:*

*Published research emerging out of computations run on the Insomnia, Ginsburg, and Manitou machines must recognize the grants that have made this service possible.*

*We ask that all related publications include the following acknowledgement text:*

*“We acknowledge computing resources from Columbia University's Shared Research Computing Facility."*

Please send citations to [hpc-support@columbia.edu](mailto:hpc-support@columbia.edu).

For more info please visit:

[https://research.columbia.edu/content/srcpac/Ginsburgpublications](https://research.columbia.edu/content/srcpac/habaneropublications)

To add publications to this listing, please email [srcpac@columbia.edu](mailto:srcpac@columbia.edu).
